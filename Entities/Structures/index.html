    Structures Overview

    Structures (defense units) form the backbone of territory control and economy in The Infinite Black 2. Any corporation that wants to survive must upgrade and maintain them they build ships, protect artifacts, and spread a sphere of influence that helps members and hurts intruders.

    Captains earn bonus Skulls for both defending and attacking non-NPC structures. Participation in structure warfare is one of the best ways to earn Skulls.

    Gear Score requirement

    Defense units must have 3,000+ Gear Score or be recalled! Equip them properly or they will be forced back.

    Corporation Limits

    Every corporation starts with one of each type and can unlock more with Tech Points (Corporation Management panel).

    Structure Start Max Notes
    Planets 1 1 Central hub; system-wide tech bonus; respawn option.
    Garrisons 1 4 Garrison Strike; only structure that can capture Relic Shrines.
    Outposts 1 8 Territory growth; movement/resist bonus for corp in range.
    Shipyards 1 3 Build ships for corp members; one faction per shipyard.

    How Defense Units Fight

    • Firepower Designed as 1015 ships worth. Most stats are 10 in attack calculations one good item on a defense is like one good item on ~15 ships.
    • Weapons & drones Attack faster than their listed speed. Slower weapons get a bigger bonus; equipping very slow weapons is highly advantageous.
    • Targeting Defenses ignore ship size targeting limits and target nearly instantly.
    • Stat multipliers All defense units: +Splash Chance 10, Battery Capacity 10, Battery Recharge 4. No 10% auxiliary item penalty like ships. Weapons and fighter drones attack 6 faster than normal.
    • Leveling Level up defense units with resources from their inspect menu.

    Defense Shields (PvP)

    When any defense unit is attacked:

    • 90-second immunity The structure (and all ships in the sector) get a 90-second shield so defenders can organize. All ships in sector are immune to PvP during this period, including attackers.
    • 1-hour shield After enough attackers die, a 1-hour protection shield is raised to give defenders a break.
    • :ALLIANCE SHIELD Alliance leaders can type this in chat to raise a 4-hour shield once per day.

    Quick Jump & Tactics

    • Quick Jump You can Quick Jump to any structure under attack except Outposts. Cost: 1 Tech Point per jump.
    • Blocking Quick Jump Engage an enemy defense unit to prevent defenders from Quick Jumping to it.

    Loot & Drops

    • NPC defenses Drop high-quality items and rare crafting components.
    • All defenses Drop hundreds or thousands of resources when destroyed.
    • Non-NPC defenses Players earn Skulls for killing them.

    PvE Deployment

    Defenses can be deployed in human-controlled PvE maps but recall automatically after 15 days. See Deployment for full rules.

    Structure Types

    Structure Summary
    Planets One per corp. Respawn option; system-wide tech; 40 Hull/Shield; Sacred Relic Shrine = alliance-wide tech.
    Garrisons Up to 4. Garrison Strike (area attack); 30 Hull/Shield; capture Relic Shrines.
    Outposts Up to 8. Territory growth every 6h; 12 Hull/Shield; bonuses for corp in territory.
    Shipyards Up to 3. Build ships; unlock blueprints; 12 Hull/Shield.

    Corporations store Artifacts at planets and garrisons for tech bonuses; any player can study an artifact for +1 Skill Point.

    NPC Factions & Behavior

    Faction Behavior Notes
    Human Non-hostile; accept deposits Garrisons at Jump Gates (Quick Jump allowed if discovered). Outposts/Shipyards roam; cannot Quick Jump to them.
    Wyrd, Hets, Precursors, Pirates Hostile Structures spawn in PvP maps and Security 9 PvE maps controlled by that faction.
