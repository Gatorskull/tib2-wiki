    Client-Extracted Crafting

    Hidden contributor staging page. Source: Assembly-CSharp\FOACJEKBFLG.cs and Assembly-CSharp\AJCLEHJNBNJ.cs.

    AJCLEHJNBNJ.GetCraftingQuality(totalCrafts, divisor, out level, out cooldownHours) calls FOACJEKBFLG.GetCraftCooldownHours(totalCrafts) and FOACJEKBFLG.GetCraftingLevel(totalCrafts, divisor). The crafting level is then converted to item quality with JCGFOGKIBPM.GetQualityType(level).

    If the divisor passed to GetCraftingLevel is greater than 1, total crafts are integer-divided by that divisor before threshold checks.
