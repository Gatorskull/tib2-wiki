    Achievements and Leaderboards

    Achievements and leaderboards track long-term progress across combat, missions, crafting, loot, events, PvP, and corporation activity. This page is a reference for what is tracked, not a strategy guide or a complete reward catalog.

    Exact milestone lists, rewards, and title unlocks still need more verification before they should be published as complete tables.

    Use This For

    Need Use
    Checking whether an activity is tracked Use the leaderboard and achievement family tables below.
    Finding related rules Follow the links to the activity, item, PvP, or structure page.
    Comparing long-term goals Use the milestone pattern notes, but treat incomplete ladders as Needs data.
    Strategy or efficient routing Use strategy pages instead of this reference page.

    Leaderboard Categories

    Category Tracked areas
    Player Achievements, major achievements, critical achievements, titles, ship XP, defense XP, resources harvested, credits earned, Spaceball credits, Spaceball Tech Points, and Party Boxes.
    Arena Arena wins, Hardcore Arena wins, arena kills, Hardcore arena kills, Arena Phoenix, and Arena Gladiator.
    Missions Solo missions, corporation missions, alliance missions, universe missions, and mission Tech Points.
    Crafting Ultimate ships, Interdictor, CPU kits, items, blueprints, ships, Assassin, Hades, Carrier, and Emperor.
    Loot Items looted, Legendary loot, Ultimate loot, relics, and fragments harvested.
    PvE NPC kills, NPC planet/garrison/outpost/shipyard kills, Pirate kills, Precursor kills, Wyrd kills, Het kills, major leader kills, minor leader kills, and Assassin leader kills.
    Invasions Invasion wins, invasion items looted, invasion leaders, and invasion bosses.
    PvP Players killed, Hardcore players killed, Skulls earned, and PvP planet/garrison/outpost/shipyard kills.

    Many leaderboard categories may also have corporation-facing versions, but each category still needs to be checked before the wiki claims a corporation leaderboard exists.

    Achievement Families

    Family Tracked examples
    Arena Arena wins and Hardcore Arena wins.
    Events Party Boxes opened, Party Boxes completed, and Rift Daily Challenge completion.
    Missions Solo, corporation, alliance, and universe mission completion.
    Corporation contribution Corporation XP contribution and corporation Tech Point contribution.
    Leader kills Major leaders, minor leaders, and Assassin leaders.
    Ships Deploying, scrapping, and building ships by faction and ship class.
    Items Scrapping, looting, repairing, upgrading, and deconstructing items by item category and rarity.
    PvP Player kills, Hardcore player kills, Skulls earned, and PvP structure kills.
    Structures Planet, Garrison, Outpost, and Shipyard kills; faction structure kills for Wyrd, Pirate, Het, and Precursor; structure repair and level-up achievements.

    Known Milestone Patterns

    Activity Known pattern
    Arena wins Dense early milestones from 1 through 20 wins, then larger milestones up to 5,000.
    Party Boxes opened Known milestones include 1, 5, 10, 25, 50, and 100 opened.
    Party Boxes completed Known milestones include 1, then 5 through 100 in larger steps, with a longer ladder through 1,000 completed.
    Rift Daily Challenge Long-tail milestones continue through 5,000 completions.
    Corporation XP contribution Known milestones include 10, 50, 100, 250, 500, 750; 1,000 through 10,000 in 500-step milestones; 20,000, 30,000, 40,000, 50,000; 100,000 through 500,000 in 100,000-step milestones; and 1,000,000.
    Leader kills Known ladders begin with 1, 5, 10, 25, 50, and 100 kills, then continue into larger long-term milestones.

    Item Achievement Axes

    Item achievements are tracked across these item categories:

    Category Related page
    Weapon Weapons
    Armor Equipment Slots
    Storage Equipment Slots
    Drone Drones
    Engine Equipment Slots
    Computer Tech Computers
    Special Special Items
    Shield Equipment Slots
    Battery Equipment Slots

    Item rarity achievements use the public rarity names: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Ultra-Rare, Elite, Legendary, and Ultimate.

    Known repair and upgrade achievement families appear to begin at Uncommon. The exact public milestone tables still need verification.
