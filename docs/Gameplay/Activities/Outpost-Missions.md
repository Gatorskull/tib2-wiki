# Outpost Missions

**Outpost Missions** are missions tied to a specific outpost. They can ask you to complete tasks such as killing specific NPCs or collecting and depositing resources.

---

## Finding the mission source

To identify which outpost or map a mission belongs to:

1. Open the **Missions** tab.
2. Click the **question mark** button.
3. Read the header above the outpost mission.

The header shows the outpost mission source, such as the map or outpost name.

---

## Mission chains

Outpost missions can chain from one task into another. For example:

1. Accept the outpost mission.
2. Complete the listed kill task, such as killing a specific NPC type.
3. Return for the next task.
4. If the next task asks for resources, collect the resource and deposit it directly into the issuing outpost.

Resource-deposit steps must be turned in at the correct outpost. Depositing at another outpost does not complete that mission step.

---

## Active mission limit

Only one outpost mission should be active at a time for the mission source you are trying to target. If you are trying to receive a specific outpost mission, avoid carrying another outpost mission that can block or confuse the chain.
