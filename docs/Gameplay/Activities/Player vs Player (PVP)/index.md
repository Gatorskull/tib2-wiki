# Player vs Player (PvP)

PvP is where you earn [**Skulls**](../../../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md), fight for territory, and help your [**alliance**](../../../Entities/Corporations/Alliances.md) capture [**Relic Shrines**](../../../Entities/Currencies/Relics.md). Human-controlled space is safe; everywhere else you can be attacked -- or attack.

## Where You're Safe (PvE Maps)

You are **safe from attack** in **human-controlled systems**, called **PvE Maps**, marked in **BLUE** on the [**Starmap**](../../../World/Maps/index.md).

When you join a [**Corporation**](../../../Entities/Corporations/index.md) you also join their **alliance**. In PvP maps you're on a team with everyone in that alliance. Friendly ships share vision, can follow or repair each other, and attack enemies to split XP and loot.

Shared target, attack, and hostile ability rules are covered on [Combat and Targeting](../../Combat-and-Targeting.md).

## Why PvP? Rewards

* **~10% more XP** and **~20% more loot** when hunting in PvP maps (vs safe space).
* [**Pity loot**](../../../Items/Pity-Loot.md) progresses faster in dangerous areas.
* [**Skulls**](../../../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md) -- from killing players and from **defending or attacking** non-NPC [**defense units**](../../../Entities/Structures/index.md). Structure warfare is a major source of Skulls.
* **Relics** -- Most PvP maps have a **Shrine**. Corps place a [**Garrison**](../../../Entities/Structures/Garrisons.md) on the shrine to capture the map and generate [**Relics**](../../../Entities/Currencies/Relics.md) for the alliance.

## Where You're (Mostly) Safe in PvP

* **At your corp's defense units** -- You're relatively safe until that structure comes under attack.
* **At Jump Gates** -- Safe only if you're **not flagged in combat**.

!!! danger "Never 100% safe"
    **You are never 100% safe in PvP maps.** Even at your own structures, an attack can start at any time.

## Collision (Stacking Limit)

A **Collision** mechanic limits how many ships can be in one sector. See [Sectors](../../../World/Maps/Sectors.md#collision-stacking-limit) for the canonical limits.

## Fleet operations

Large PvP fights use **drivers**, **assist leaders**, and shared **sort calls** as gameplay/UI concepts. See [Fleet PvP](Fleet-PvP.md) for the rules summary and [Fleet PvP Quick Start](../../../Getting-Started/Quick-Start-Guides/Fleet-PvP-Quick-Start.md) for cattabliss' practical guide.

## Love Day (No Open-World PvP)

**Intergalactic Love Day** is a daily event that **disables open-world PvP**. On Love Days everyone can work together. It alternates with **War Day** (PvP on) at each daily reset (00:00 UTC).

## PvP Activities

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| [**The Arena**](Arena.md) | Controlled PvP matches every few hours; awards Skulls and has no ship durability loss. |
| [**Fleet PvP**](Fleet-PvP.md) | Open-world organized PvP with drivers, assist leaders, and sort calls. |
| **Open-world PvP** | Fight enemy players in PvP maps. |
| [**Structure Warfare Rules**](Structure Busts.md) | Attack or defend [defense units](../../../Entities/Structures/index.md); bonus Skulls for both. |
