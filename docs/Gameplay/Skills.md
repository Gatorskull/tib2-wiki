# Skills

**Captains**, [**Ships**](../Ships/index.md), [**Corporations**](../Entities/Corporations/index.md), and defense structures each have a **Skill Tree**. Points invested give permanent stat improvements -- and **all of these stack** (captain + corp + ship).

New skill points come from leveling and from studying [Artifacts](../Entities/Structures/Artifacts.md). Captains study artifacts captured at corporation [planets](../Entities/Structures/Planets.md) and garrisons.

Skills can be invested up to **10 points per stat**. The bonus is reduced above **5 points**.

[Hardcore ships and defense units](../Ships/Hardcore-Units.md) gain bonus skill points and can invest an additional **5 max points per stat**.

Like stats from [items](../Items/index.md) and [technology](Technology.md), skill bonuses are subject to [diminishing returns](../Items/Diminishing-Returns.md).
