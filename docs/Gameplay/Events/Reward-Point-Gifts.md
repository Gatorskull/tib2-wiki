# Reward Point Gifts

**Reward Points** are earned by supporting the game and by certain in-game actions. They unlock bonus gifts (Party Boxes, modules, skins, and more). Open **Captain Panel → REWARDS** for a summary of your points and prizes.

## How to Earn Reward Points

| Source | Reward Points |
|--------|----------------|
| **Tech Point purchases** | Every **1 [Tech Point](/Entities/Currencies/Tech%20Points.md)** = **1 Reward Point**. Buy at [Spellbook.com](https://www.spellbook.com/purchase/tib2/techpoints.php). |
| **Destroying NPC defense structures** | **Planets:** 25. **Garrisons:** 8. **Outposts or Shipyards:** 4. |
| **Open-world PvP** | Making the **killing shot** in [PvP](/Gameplay/Activities/Player%20vs%20Player%20(PVP)/index.md) (non-Arena). |

## Gifts You Can Claim

| Reward | Cost | Notes |
|--------|------|--------|
| [**Deception Module**](/Ships/Ship-Mods.md) | 1,000 | Hides gear and stats from other players; works with other ship mods. |
| **Captain title** | — | Exclusive title. |
| **Retro Ship Skins** | — | Exclusive [skins](/Ships/index.md). |
| **Special random bonus** | 5,000 | Unlock at 5,000 Reward Points. |
| Re-Mutate Item Kit | 3,000 | Upgrades and re-rolls a mutated item (Captain Panel → REWARDS). |
| [**Party Boxes**](Party-Boxes.md) | — | Use to gift corp or alliance activities and combat bonuses. |

!!! info "Supporting the game"
    Tech Point purchases help cover server and development costs. Reward Points are a way to thank players for continued support.
