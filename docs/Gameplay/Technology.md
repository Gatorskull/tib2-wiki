# Technology

There are **over 250 technologies** in TIB2 that affect combat, movement, crafting, and more. They come from Artifacts, corporation research, special items, and alien ships.

* **Ranked tech** (e.g. Improved Hulls) does not stack with lower ranks.
* **Non-ranked tech** (e.g. Analysis, Targeting) stacks with other similar benefits.

Some low-level technology can be boosted for one hour using [**Tech Points**](../Entities/Currencies/Tech Points.md). The timer does not count down while that technology is superseded by a better or similar technology, such as corporation or artifact technology.

## Stacking and item slots

Technology from items applies at full strength even when the item is in an auxiliary [equipment slot](../Items/Equipment-Slots.md). The 10% auxiliary-slot rule affects item stats, not the technology attached to the item.

Corporation technology, artifact technology, item technology, and ship/faction technology can all contribute to a build, subject to the ranked/non-ranked stacking rules above.
