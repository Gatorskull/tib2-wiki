# Technology

There are **over 250 technologies** in TIB2 that affect combat, movement, crafting, and more. They come from Artifacts, corporation research, special items, and alien ships.

* **Ranked tech** (e.g. Improved Hulls) does not stack with lower ranks.
* **Non-ranked tech** (e.g. Analysis, Targeting) stacks with other similar benefits.

In-game: **HELP menu → INFORMATION → TECHNOLOGY LIST** for the full list.
