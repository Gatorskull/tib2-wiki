# Abilities

Hotbar abilities and special technologies that shape combat: **Grapple**, **Hack**, **Drain**, **Technician** (repairs), **Taunt** (tank), **Deflector**, and **Junker**. Many hostile abilities use the same stat-vs-resist pattern: the matching Chance stat strengthens the effect, the matching Resist stat weakens it, and player Hit Chance/Evasion can contribute.

Shared target, cooldown, battery, and hostile-action gates live on [Combat and Targeting](../Combat-and-Targeting.md).

## Hotbar Abilities

Grapple, Hack, and Drain require **10+** of the matching stat to use, require a target in the same sector, and have a default **50 s** cooldown. Grapple and Hack cost battery; Drain does not.

| Ability | Stat | Effect | See |
|---------|------|--------|-----|
| **Grapple** | Grapple Chance | Target loses 25 Evasion and cannot move; costs battery. | [Grapple](Grapple.md) |
| **Hack** | Hack Chance | Target can't use hotbar (except Repair), no battery regen, no special movement; costs battery. | [Hacking](Hacking.md) |
| **Drain** | Drain Chance | Steals shields and battery; no battery cost to use. | [Drain](Drain.md) |

## Special Items & Tech

| Topic | Source | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| [**Technician**](Technician.md) | Technician Special item or Medic ship mod | Repair upgrades: purge, critical/splash repair, repair-over-time (immune to Grapple/Hack/Taunt). |
| [**Taunt**](Taunt.md) | Tank Special (Ultra-Rare+) | Forces damaged enemies to attack the tank; Technician can remove. |
| [**Deflector**](Deflector.md) | Deflector tech | Chance to negate 100% damage and return it to attacker. |
| [**Junker**](Junker.md) | Junker tech | Hull repair to attacker when they deal damage (33-100%; no Technician bonus). |
