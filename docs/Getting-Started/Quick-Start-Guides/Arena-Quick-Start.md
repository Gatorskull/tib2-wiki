# Arena Quick Start

*By cattabliss. LuckyMFer helped with the arena section; see [Lucky's Arena Notes](Lucky-Arena-Notes.md).*

**What do I do in arena** Arena is a game mode that activates once per hour, at the top of the hour. (example: 00:00, 04:00, etc) Typically, players are given 3 minutes to register. You can register for arena by clicking your player name, then clicking arena, during the registration window. You will be given the chance to select anywhere from two to four ships to participate in the arena.

Once you enter arena, you will be placed onto a random team. Carriers do not work in arena, and it is almost a requirement that all ships are geared in a way to allow them to move at least at 3.5 seconds per move, or faster. This can be achieved by using movespeed void gear, using a crux drive, or a skip drive.

**Fleet arenas** allow follows and generally is made up of three or fewer teams. Each team will typically designate a driver by linking a ship in chat. Follow that ship.

**Chaos arenas** are typically very small teams, spread out all over the arena battleground.

**Standard arenas** are typically a few big fleets, but there is no follow permitted, and everyone must move on their own.

**Ultimate arenas** are a modifier on the above three arenas, except all your gear turns into ultimate rarity for the duration of the arena.

**TIPS:** The best items you can bring as a new player to the arena is a crux drive, and/or a stalker. The crux allows you to keep up with the rest, the stalker will allow you to go invisible when targeted, extending your survival.

## Arena Requirements

You must have a minimum of 1,500 gear score to receive full rewards in the arena. No "new player items" are permitted in the arena. You must do a minimum of approximately 1,500 damage to gain skulls in arena. In general, losing an arena will reward you with 7 skulls, while winning will get you 15, with more skulls being awarded to team MVPs who deal the most damage, make the most repairs, have the most abilities used, or make the most kills.

## Arena Gear

Total hull or shield is one of the most important assets in arena. Small HP pools get targeted first. One trick some players use is to bring a very large shield, which allows you to wear a small hull and possibly maintain a 3.5s ship using cheap movespeed items. Your other option is to obtain a crux drive or skip drive (depletable, cannot be repaired outside of upgrade bonuses) and wear a large hull.

I recommend a hull or shield pool of at least 8,000, even 10,000 if possible. It will help you survive longer. The other parts would be your guns and your fighters. Make sure you've got them equipped and your offensive stats -- crit chance, hit chance, damage/hull damage buff -- will help you do sufficient damage.

Lastly, your special in arena is probably the most important item you can bring. An advanced shield with a 12k shield can make it significantly larger, making you less likely to be targeted. A technician can help heal and degrapple yourself to escape. A stalker is extremely cheap and can help you vanish if you get focus targeted. Consider your choice and make a strategy to survive and do damage.
