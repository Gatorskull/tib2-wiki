# Tech Points Quick Start

*By cattabliss.*

**What are tech points (TP)? What should I spend them on?** Tech Points (TP) are one of the three basic currencies in The Infinite Black 2. You will acquire TP through in-game achievements, completing missions or purchasing them with credits (1,000 credits = 1 TP) or real life money on tib2.com. TP can be purchased with credits, but cannot be sold or traded. They have a few functions:

- **Augmenting items**: increasing an item's rarity. The cost to increase an item's rarity increases with rarity. An uncommon special costs 18 tech points to increase in rarity to rare, a rare costs 36 TP to augment to ultra rare, an ultra rare costs 240 TP to increase to an elite, and so on. In general, only items of at least 230+ quality at uncommon rarity are worth augmenting. To augment an item, click the item and click "upgrade".
- **Ranking up/down items**: some items can be increased or decreased in size, including: weapons, shields, storages, armors. Increasing an item's size will generally increase the item's positive and negative stats. The main reason you will want to increase or decrease an item's size is to maximize the use of your ship's slots. To rank an item, click the item and click "upgrade" -- it is in the same menu as augment.
- **Teleporting**: as long as you are not flagged in combat, you may teleport to any NPC garrison that you have "studied" and any corp/alliance structure, regardless of whether you have visited the corp/alliance structure. Each teleport costs 1 TP. Teleporting can be accessed in the three line "hamburger" menu in the top left.
- **Repairs**: there are two ways to repair items. One way is to click "upgrade" on any item's menu, and hitting repair. However, this first method will reduce the quality of your item. Another way is to use a chat code `:corp repair mytp confirm` which will repair 5 random equipped items by 10% of every online player in the corporation, at an approximate cost of 1.5 tech points per item.
