# Item Repair Quick Start

*By cattabliss.*

**Repairing items: how to not lose quality** There are 2 primary ways to repair items in TIB2.

**1)** The first way is through the item upgrade menu. Click any item, click upgrade, and hit repair. This will subtract a small number of Tech points from your wallet and repair the item to full durability at the cost of a small amount of quality. The vast majority of players do not use this method, as quality, once reduced, can typically not be regained, meaning that the item will be reduced in quality and value every time it is repaired. Repairing an item with at least 90% durability using this method does not reduce quality.

**2)** The second way is the most common method players use to repair their durability, without losing any quality at all. You may have seen "alliance" and "corp" repairs pop up in the chat, where a few of your random items are repaired by 10% by other members of your corp/alliance. These repairs cost roughly 1.5 tech points per item being repaired, and will repair either 2 (alliance) or 5 (corp) items for every member online.

To trigger a corp repair (repairs 5 items per player by 10% of every player online in corp), type in chat:

```text
:corp repair mytp confirm
```

To trigger an alliance repair (repairs 2 items per player by 10% of every player online in alliance), type in chat:

```text
:alliance repair mytp confirm
```

TP will be automatically subtracted from your wallet. Be aware that if there are many players online, this may cost you a substantial amount of TP. Many players wait until there is almost no one online to spam these repairs on the cheap.
