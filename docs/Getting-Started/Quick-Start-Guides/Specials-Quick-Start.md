# Specials Quick Start

*By cattabliss.*

**Specials and what do they do** Specials are an item slot. Like other items, they are affected by quality and by whether they have "void" status. Unlike other items, specials cannot have their quality repaired/increased through in-game quality kits. **Specials generally only realize their full potential when ranked to ultimate.** The following is a list of specials in the game, ranked by no particular order:

- **Technician**: probably the best special in the game. An ultimate variant of this allows your repairs to heal 1,200 hull points per click, have a chance to crit for 2,400 hull points of heal, plus splash up to four other ships with heal, and also apply a repair-over-time status to any ship affected by its heal. Ships affected by repair-over-time are immune to all status effects for approximately 10 seconds, except for advanced hack 3, which disables abilities.
- **Prospector:** the perfect farming special. Normally, harvesting an asteroid or debris grants 1 resource per # size of an asteriod. A roid listed as 12/12 will loot up to 12 resources. A prospector gives a chance to create bonus resources, potentially causing that 12/12 rock to yield 40 resources or more. Pair it with harvesters for max goodness.
- **Advanced Munitions**: the best special to make your gun do more damage. It adds critical damage, raw damage, splash chance. Pair it with a high crit ship.
- **Scout**: The special that gives you a little bit of speed, and the power to see what is around you. Ultimate scout specials can decloak any ship.
- **Stalker**: The opposite of a scout. This special makes you invisible for a short amount of time and can be useful as a method to escape when being targeted or chased. You can only be decloaked if you are scanned by a more powerful scanner, if the cloak timer runs out, or if you fire your weapon.
- **Advanced Propulsion:** the special to make you move fast. Most commonly found on carriers.
- **Alien Technology:** the most basic of specials. Add +1 EP, not much else. Generally only used by those under lv 10. Otherwise, its usefulness is for deconstruction, to create new special blueprints.
- **Alien Hunter:** a special that helps you generate more loot when killing NPCs. This special affects the loot rate modifier, meaning more resources, more frequent and better drops, and is stackable. In general, the loot rate modifier is maxed in the presence of an ulimate alien hunter, or a combination of lower rarity alien hunters.
- **Hacker**: Hacker special ranked to ultimate allows you to use hack to disable an enemy ship's abilities roughly once every 35 seconds. An ultimate hack can remove a technician's repair-over-time and stop a crux drive from functioning.
- **Grappling Hook**: a hook ranked to ultimate allows you to "drag" an enemy with you into the next sector. It is an effective PVP item used commonly in medium or smaller ships. The drag ability only works at ultimate rarity.
- **Nova Device**: a device that makes it so, when your ship dies, you explode after a short delay, damaging all non-friendly ships around you. Nova can be disabled via advanced hack.
- **Deflector**: most commonly used in structures, gives a % chance to deflect the damage shot at you into damage onto the person shooting at you.
- **Vampire**: drains shields and batteries. Nothing special happens at ultimate. This special is not really used.
- **Battle Ram:** one of the best specials for a new player. Creates % chance of a ram, causing large damage, when moving. Ram kills do not reward XP, but rams can be used to lower the health of an NPC ship significantly, allowing you to finish it in a few shots for full XP.
- **Advanced Electronics:** a combat special that adds resistances and critical chance. It is a lesser used special as it offers no competitive advantages at ultimate rarity.
- **Advanced Construct:** a support special that increases EP, hull points and other soft stats, while granting immunity to ram type attacks.
- **Drone Master**: Increases damage and heals done by drones, and reduces ship target speed. Not commonly used.
- **Advanced Shields:** Increases shields by a percentage in addition to a stat bonus, plus increases to other defensive resist stats.
- **Tank**: Decreases damage output in exchange for increased hull and taunt ability, which forces an enemy ship to target you. Damage output debuff can be nearly negated at 256 void ultimate.
