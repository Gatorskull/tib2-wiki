# Credits Quick Start

*By cattabliss.*

!!! note "Market-sensitive guide"
    This page preserves community guide advice. Any specific credit values are historical guide context, not current market prices.

**How do I get more credits?** Credits are one of the three main currencies, alongside skulls and tech points, are the best asset in all of TIB2. TIB2 is a deflationary economy, this means credit really is king, and holding onto credit means you will likely be able to buy more tomorrow with your money than you can today, as items become more common and their value decreases relative to the main currency. There are a few ways to get more credits:

- Scrap items. Almost everything you can loot in the game that is not void, and not ultimate, should be scrapped, unless it is better than your current equipped items. There is generally no reason to keep an excess amount of low quality gear. Scrap it and turn it into money, which will buy you more gear later.
- Scrap resources. For those who played TIB1, you might be confused by how resources are not automatically sold into credits, and are instead deposited. You can access your deposited resources by clicking your player name menu > crafting > stacks > creating stacks. The created stack of resources can then be sold onto the market, or scrapped for credits. Metals are the most commonly dropped resource, and are the best to scrap for credits.
- Sell items on the market. Any player can list almost any item (with some limited exceptions) onto the market, for any price as an immediate buyout, or as an auction with a starting bid and an optional buy-out price. TIB2 does not have a market history, so the best way to figure out what to list an item for is to ask.
- Join planet / structure busts Higher level players will regularly make calls in alliance chat for "planet" or "uni/structure/clear" busts, typically linking a driver ship and a location to jump to. Attending these is one of the most effective ways to gain credits -- each structure kill nets a UR item worth 2,000+ credit scrap, and each planet nets a legendary item worth 5,000+ scrap. 7,000+ hull is recommended for busts.
