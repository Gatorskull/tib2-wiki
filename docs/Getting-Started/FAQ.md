# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from new and returning players. If you don't see your question here, check [How To Play](How-To-Play.md) or type **:HELP** in chat.

---

### I'm a new player! How do I play? What should I do and where do I start?

Start with [**How To Play**](How-To-Play.md), then use [Common Workflows](Common-Workflows.md) when you need exact click paths.

---

### This game is hard -- How do I get better?

Combat is balanced around **multiple ships**. Playing with a team and deploying additional ships is critical to success!

1. Explore **100% of the Sol Star System** to earn your first ship.
2. Click **"Deploy 2nd Ship"** to grow your fleet.
3. Join a [**Corporation**](../Entities/Corporations/index.md) -- they provide [**Technology**](../Gameplay/Technology.md), [**Skills**](../Gameplay/Skills.md), and group protection.

### What settings should I turn on?

Turn on **Display local sector ship counts** early. It helps you avoid collision in fleet play and read friendly/enemy presence in a sector. See [Recommended Settings](Settings.md) for the rest of the new-player settings pass.

### What are drones?

See [**Drones**](../Items/Drones.md) for Fighter, Harvester, and Repair drone roles, and [Equipment Slots](../Items/Equipment-Slots.md) for first-slot vs auxiliary-slot behavior.

### What skills should I get first?

For the rules, see [Skills](../Gameplay/Skills.md): corporation, captain, and ship skills all stack. For recommendations, ask your corporation or use the player-written quick starts and strategy pages.

---

### How do I get more Ship XP, Player XP, or Corp XP?

[**Technology**](../Gameplay/Technology.md), [**Skills**](../Gameplay/Skills.md), and [**Items**](../Items/index.md) all stack to give your ships a **+XP% Bonus**. When you kill an NPC you gain XP based on your ship's level vs the target's level. A share of that goes to your player, and a share to your corporation -- so boosting ship XP boosts all three.

---

### What is Ship Durability?

When ships are destroyed they can lose a small amount of **durability**. Repair ship durability with [**Skulls**](../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md) using the **REPAIR** button on the ship inspection panel. See [Stats](../Items/Stats.md) for affected stat definitions.

---

### How do I get Skulls?

Skulls are earned only from [**Player vs Player Combat**](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/index.md>). See the [**Skulls**](../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md) article for details and [**The Arena**](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Arena.md>) as the main way to earn them.

---

### What is Item Durability?

---

### What ship should I build first?

Ship construction rules are on [Ship Crafting](../Ships/Ship-Crafting.md), and ship requirements are on [Ship Reference](../Ships/Ship-Reference.md). Build recommendations belong in your corporation's advice, [Ship Building Strategy](../Strategy/Ship-Building.md), or [Build Philosophy](../Strategy/Build-Philosophy.md).

---

### Why do my ship stats seem lower than they should be?

See [**Stat Bonus Diminishing Returns**](../Items/Diminishing-Returns.md). Stats use diminishing returns so stacking one stat gives less benefit the more you have.
