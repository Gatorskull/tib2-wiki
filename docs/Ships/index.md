# Ships

Your fleet is the heart of *The Infinite Black 2*. You command multiple ships, each with its own equipment, level, and role. Combat is balanced around **multiple ships**. New-player routing belongs in [Getting Started](../Getting-Started/index.md).

## Ship Classes

There are **10 ship types** in TIB2:

| Size | Classes |
|------|---------|
| **Small** | Shuttle, Frigate, Assassin |
| **Medium** | Cruiser, Destroyer |
| **Large** | Battleship, Flagship |
| **Huge** | Carrier, Hades |
| **Massive** | Emperor |

Each of the **5 factions** (Human, Wyrd, Het, Precursor, Pirate) has its own variant of each class -- **50 different ships** in total. [**Ship size**](Size-And-Targeting.md) controls movement speed, targeting speed, [**Equip Points**](Equip-Points.md), and more.

Carriers become available at **level 35** and can lead a fleet using [Carrier Leadership](Carrier-Leadership.md). Carrier Leadership requires corporation membership and lets the fleet inherit the Carrier's movement pacing.

## Fleet Size Requirements

Your fleet must contain a **mix of ship sizes** to deploy more slots:

| Fleet size | Requirement |
|------------|-------------|
| 8 ships | At least one deployed ship must be Medium or smaller |
| 9 ships | At least two deployed ships must be Medium or smaller |
| 10 ships | At least two deployed ships must be Medium or smaller |

Small ships also count as Medium-or-smaller. See [**Small Strike Team Bonus**](../Gameplay/Abilities/Small-Strike-Team-Bonus.md) for the benefit of fielding small/medium ships.

## Starting Ship

Players begin in a **Striker Gunboat**. Striker Gunboats gain more equipment slots and Equip Points with [**Elite Ranks**](Elite-Ranks.md). You can own **100+ ships** of any type and deploy several at once.
