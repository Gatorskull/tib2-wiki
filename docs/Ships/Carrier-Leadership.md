# Carrier Leadership

**Carrier Leadership** lets a Carrier act as the movement anchor for a fleet. Once a player can use a Carrier, the fleet becomes much easier to move and manage.

!!! info "Corporation required"
    Carrier Leadership only works while the player is in a corporation.

---

## When carriers unlock

Players can use a **Carrier** at **level 35**.

For many pilots, this is one of the first major comfort milestones: the Carrier can be built for speed while slower damage ships follow and inherit the Carrier's movement pacing.

---

## Fleet movement

When a Carrier leads a fleet, the rest of the fleet inherits the Carrier's movement pacing.

Carrier Leadership is a rule page. Build advice belongs in [Build Philosophy](../Strategy/Build-Philosophy.md) or another strategy page.

---

## Move speed limits

Ship size sets a hard minimum move time. Extra move speed does not make a ship move below its size minimum, but it can help counter enemy [Interdictor](Interdictor-Carrier.md) penalties.

See [Size and Targeting](Size-And-Targeting.md#minimum-move-times) for the canonical size table.

---

## Pre-jump animation

Each ship also has a **pre-jump animation** that increases with size. This is separate from the move-speed cap. See [Size and Targeting](Size-And-Targeting.md#movement-warm-up) for warm-up times by size.
