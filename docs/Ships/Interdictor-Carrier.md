# Interdictor Carrier

The **Interdictor** is a [**Ship Mod**](Ship-Mods.md) that is **crafted** from your captain's Item Crafting panel. It allows a **Carrier-class** ship to become an **Interdictor**, permanently **replacing** the Carrier's Leadership bonus with a sector-wide penalty to enemies.

[**Gravity Drives**](../Items/Gravity-Drives.md) negate the Interdictor's movement penalties for ships that have them equipped.

---

## What the Interdictor Does

- **Sector penalty** -- The Interdictor projects an effect in **nearby sectors** that:
  - **Increases** enemy **targeting** and **movement** times by **15% / 20% / 25% / 30%** by Interdictor rank.
  - **Removes** other movement benefits such as **Space Lanes**.
- **One active source per sector** -- Only **one** Interdictor source can apply the active penalty in a sector at a time. Other Interdictors in the affected sector do not stack the penalty or share control.
- **No effect in The Arena** -- The Interdictor effect does **not** work in [**The Arena**](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Arena.md>).

| Interdictor rank | Movement and targeting penalty |
|---|---:|
| I | +15% |
| II | +20% |
| III | +25% |
| IV | +30% |

---

## Map Indicators

Interdictor influence is visible in the game world:

- **Enemy Interdictor influence** appears as a bright red, pulsing sector indicator. Your ships are penalized in that sector.
- **Friendly Interdictor influence** appears as a light gray pulsing ring. Enemies are penalized there.

---

## Crafting

- Interdictor is **crafted** like the [Ultimate](Ship-Mods.md) ship mod.
- Requires **identical fragments and Relics all from the same faction**.
- The **final crafted item** is the same regardless of faction; only the source materials must match.
