# Items

Ships and structures equip **items** to gain stats, weapons, and abilities. Loot drops, crafting, and rewards all provide gear.

## Item Types

There are **9 equipment types** in TIB2:

| Type     | Purpose                                  | Notes                                                                                                                            |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Weapon   | Damage output                            | Uses targeting and usually consumes battery.                                                                                     |
| Armor    | Hull and defense                         | Can be ranked up or down.                                                                                                        |
| Shield   | Shield capacity and resists              | Can be ranked up or down.                                                                                                        |
| Battery  | Power capacity and recharge              | Extra battery slots use auxiliary-slot rules.                                                                                    |
| Storage  | Resource capacity                        | Can be ranked up or down.                                                                                                        |
| Drone    | Fighter, Repair, or Harvester            | Main drone actions work at full strength in auxiliary slots. See [Drones](Drones.md).                                            |
| Engine   | Movement and special movement            | Affects travel and movement builds.                                                                                              |
| Computer | Targeting and utility                    | Often supports accuracy, lock speed, and ability stats. See [Tech Computers](Tech-Computers.md) for CPU deconstruction upgrades. |
| Special  | Unique abilities (e.g. Technician, Tank) | Often unlocks technology-like effects. See [Special Items](Specials.md).                                                         |

**Gear Score** is the sum of your equipped items' scores (based on rarity, quality, rank). Many activities (e.g. [**The Arena**](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Arena.md>)) require a minimum Gear Score.

For weapons, drones, and batteries, the first slot gives full item stats while auxiliary slots give reduced stats. See [Equipment Slots](Equipment-Slots.md).

Weapons, armor, shields, and storage have **Rank**. Changing rank modifies both the item's power and its [Equip Point](../Ships/Equip-Points.md) cost. Rarity, quality, rank, and repairs are covered on [Item Upgrades](Item-Upgrades.md). Restrictions for repair, scrap, deconstruction, auction, and corp-bank actions are covered on [Item Management](Item-Management.md).

## Drones

Drones have three roles: **Fighter**, **Repair**, and **Harvester**. See [Drones](Drones.md) for drone roles, faction differences, action timing, Equip Point cost, and Drone Master notes.
