# Equipment Slots

Ships can equip multiple items of some types, such as weapons, drones, and batteries. The **first slot** and **auxiliary slots** do not behave the same way.

---

## Main slot vs auxiliary slots

For **weapons**, **drones**, and **batteries**:

| Slot | Item stats | Main item action |
|------|------------|------------------|
| First slot | 100% of item stats | 100% |
| Auxiliary slots | 10% of item stats | 100% |

Auxiliary slots give only **10% of all stats** on that item.

The item's main action still works at full strength:

- Weapons still shoot at 100%.
- Fighter drones still attack at 100%.
- Repair drones still heal at 100%.
- Harvester drones still harvest at 100%.

Technologies from items apply at full strength even when the item is in an auxiliary slot.

---

## What auxiliary scaling affects

Auxiliary scaling affects the item's stat package, not the item type's main action.

Examples:

- A weapon in an auxiliary slot still fires normally, but its stat bonuses are reduced.
- A repair drone in an auxiliary slot still repairs normally, but its stat bonuses are reduced.
- A harvester drone in an auxiliary slot still harvests normally, but its stat bonuses are reduced.
- A battery in an auxiliary slot still functions as a battery, but its stat bonuses are reduced.

For build advice about which item belongs in which slot, see [Build Philosophy](../Strategy/Build-Philosophy.md) or [Ship Building Strategy](../Strategy/Ship-Building.md).
