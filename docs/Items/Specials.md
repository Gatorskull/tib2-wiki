# Special Items

**Specials** are an [item](index.md) type that can add unique abilities, technology lines, or unusual stat packages. Like other items, Specials are affected by [quality](Item-Upgrades.md), [Void](Void-Items.md) status, and [mutation](Mutate-Item-Kit.md). Unlike most item types, Special quality may not be repairable upward with normal in-game quality kits.

Many Specials do not show their full behavior until high rarity or **Ultimate** rarity. This page is a reference index; build advice belongs in [Build Philosophy](../Strategy/Build-Philosophy.md) or the cattabliss [Specials Quick Start](../Getting-Started/Quick-Start-Guides/Specials-Quick-Start.md).

## Stat Package Specials

These Specials mainly add stat packages or broad technology lines. Exact stat packages should not be published until verified by rarity.

| Special | General role |
|---------|--------------|
| **Advanced Munitions** | Offensive stats such as damage, critical damage, and splash chance. |
| **Advanced Electronics** | Combat utility and resistance-style stats. |
| **Advanced Propulsion** | Movement-focused stats; often associated with fast ships. |
| **Advanced Construct** | Support stats such as Equip Points and hull, plus immunity to ram-type attacks. |
| **Advanced Shields** | Shield percentage and defensive resist stats. |
| **Alien Technology** | Basic Special that adds Equip Points and can be deconstructed for Special blueprints. |
| **Weird Alien Artifact** | Artifact-style Special with special restrictions in some contexts, such as Hardcore Arena. |

## Shared Rules and Interactions

- [Advanced Hacking III](../Gameplay/Abilities/Hacking.md) can disable Special item abilities such as Nova Device and Deflector.
- [Auxiliary slot](Equipment-Slots.md) rules apply if a ship has more than one slot of a type. Technologies from auxiliary items apply in full, while normal item stats are reduced according to aux rules.
- Rarity, rank, quality, Void, and mutation behavior are documented on [Item Upgrades](Item-Upgrades.md), [Void Items](Void-Items.md), and [Mutate Item Kit](Mutate-Item-Kit.md).
- Item action restrictions such as repair, deconstruction, trade, auction, and item lock are documented on [Item Management](Item-Management.md).
