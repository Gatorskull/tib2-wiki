# Factions

The [Starmap](../Maps/index.md) is controlled by **five factions**. Each has its own ship variants, homeworld, and lore. Human-controlled space is safe; the other four factions control systems where [PvP](<../../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/index.md>) is possible and where [corporations](../../Entities/Corporations/index.md) fight for territory and [Relics](../../Entities/Currencies/Relics.md).

## Faction Lore

| Faction | Summary | Full article |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| **Wyrd** | Spider-like species; venom, mandibles, possible telepathy; transformed the Hets. | [Wyrd](Wyrd.md) |
| **Het** | Once docile, now aggressive; hive-mind pods; weaponized by Wyrd; organo-metallic. | [Het](Het.md) |
| **Precursor** | Ancient, robotic forms; conflict with The Oblivion; cryptic guardians. | [Precursor](Precursor.md) |
| **Pirates** | Humans who fled Earth; corporate vs pirate; clones, codes of honor. | [Pirates](Pirates.md) |
| **Humans** | Corporate and EDF; Sol and colonial space; safe PvE space. | -- |

Each faction has a **homeworld** at (7, 7) in its Security 9 map -- see [Planets](../../Entities/Structures/Planets.md). [Shipyards](../../Entities/Structures/Shipyards.md) can specialize in one faction for ship building.

## Mechanical Differences

Faction affects ships, items, structures, and crafting:

| Faction | Stable mechanical identity |
|---------|----------------------------|
| **Human** | Lower ship level requirements, cheaper advanced-ship Skull costs, safe human space, and several baseline item families. |
| **Wyrd** | Extra battery-slot patterns on ships, Wyrd shield/drone/item families, and Wyrd defense affinity techs around Shield Capacitors and Advanced Hacking. |
| **Pirate** | Pirate ship/item families, Pirate defense affinity techs around Advanced Alloys and Attack Drones, and battery bonuses that can apply broadly to Pirate ships. |
| **Het** | Extra drone-slot patterns on ships, Het shield/drone/item families, and Het defense affinity techs around Shield Pierce and Technician. |
| **Precursor** | Higher ship requirements and base EP trend, extra weapon-slot patterns, Precursor shield/drone/item families, and Precursor defense affinity techs around Shield Amplifier and Advanced Draining. |

For exact ship slot, Equip Point, crafting, item, and defense rules, use the dedicated rules pages instead of repeating those tables here.

Practical faction ship advice belongs in [Faction Ship Notes](../../Strategy/Faction-Ship-Notes.md).
