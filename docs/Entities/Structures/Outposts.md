# Outposts

**Outposts** expand territory and support corp members in the area. Each corporation can have up to **8 Outposts**.

## Territory & Bonuses

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Territory growth** | Outposts grow a **sphere of influence** by **1 sector every 6 hours**, up to a maximum of **(2 + Outpost_Level ÷ 5)** sectors. |
| **Corp members in territory** | **Movement** and **resist** bonuses. |
| **Enemies in territory** | **Slower** and **less protected**. |
| **Hull/Shield** | **+Max Hull** and **+Max Shield** stats are multiplied by **×12**. |

!!! info "Quick Jump"
    You **cannot** Quick Jump to Outposts (including when they’re under attack). You can Quick Jump to Planets, Garrisons, and Shipyards.

## Human Outposts (PvE)

* **Roaming:** Human Outposts **randomly roam** maps.
* **Travel:** You cannot Quick Jump to Human Outposts.
* **Services:** Non-hostile; they accept resource deposits.

## Deployment
