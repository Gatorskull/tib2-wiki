# Outposts

**Outposts** expand territory and support corp members in the area. Each corporation can have up to **8 Outposts**.

## Territory & Bonuses

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Territory growth** | Outposts grow a **sphere of influence** by **1 sector every 6 hours**, up to a maximum of **(2 + Outpost_Level / 5)** sectors. |
| **Corp members in territory** | **Movement** and **resist** bonuses. |
| **Enemies in territory** | **Slower** and **less protected**. |
| **Hull/Shield** | **+Max Hull** and **+Max Shield** stats are multiplied by **x12**. |

!!! info "Quick Jump"
    Attack-response Quick Jump appears to exclude Outposts, but player-owned alliance Outpost edge cases need confirmation. Keep Outpost jump rules linked to [Maps](../../World/Maps/index.md#quick-jump) and [Structure Warfare Rules](<../../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Structure Busts.md>) until verified in game.

## Human Outposts (PvE)

* **Roaming:** Human Outposts **randomly roam** maps.
* **Travel:** Human Outposts are not discovered Jump Gate targets.
* **Services:** Non-hostile; they accept resource deposits.

## Deployment

* **Cost:** 15 [Tech Points](../Currencies/Tech Points.md).
* **Rules:** See [Deployment](Deployment.md).
