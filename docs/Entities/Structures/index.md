# Structures Overview

Structures, also called defense units, are corporation-owned entities used for territory, ship construction, artifacts, relics, and structure warfare.

Captains earn [**Skulls**](../Currencies/Skulls.md) for **both defending and attacking** non-NPC structures.

!!! danger "Gear Score requirement"
    **Defense units must have 3,000+ [Gear Score](../../Items/index.md) or be recalled!** Equip them properly or they will be forced back.

---

## Corporation Limits

Every corporation starts with one of each type and can unlock more with [**Tech Points**](../Currencies/Tech Points.md) (Corporation Management panel).

| Structure   | Start | Max | Notes |
|-------------|:-----:|:---:|-------|
| **Planets** | 1     | 1   | Central hub; system-wide tech bonus; respawn option. |
| **Garrisons** | 1   | 4   | Garrison Strike; only structure that can capture [Relic Shrines](../Currencies/Relics.md). |
| **Outposts** | 1    | 8   | Territory growth; movement/resist bonus for corp in range. |
| **Shipyards** | 1   | 4   | Build [ships](../../Ships/index.md) for corp members; one faction per shipyard. |

## How Defense Units Fight

* **Firepower** -- Defense units are much stronger than a single ship. Many defense-unit stats use large multipliers.
* **Weapons & drones** -- Attack **faster** than their listed speed. Slower weapons receive a larger attack-speed bonus.
* **Targeting** -- Defenses **ignore** [ship size](../../Ships/index.md) targeting limits and target nearly instantly.
* **Stat multipliers** -- All defense units: **+Splash Chance x10**, **Battery Capacity x10**, **Battery Recharge x4**. No 10% auxiliary item penalty like ships. Weapons and fighter drones attack **x6** faster than normal.
* **Leveling** -- Level up defense units with **resources** from their inspect menu.

---

## Defense Shields (PvP)

When any defense unit is **attacked**:

* **90-second immunity** -- The structure (and all ships in the sector) get a 90-second shield so defenders can organize. All ships in sector are immune to PvP during this period, including attackers.
* **1-hour shield** -- After enough **attackers** die, a 1-hour protection shield is raised to give defenders a break.
* **:ALLIANCE SHIELD** -- Alliance leaders can type this in chat to raise a **4-hour shield** **once per day**.

## Loot & Drops

* **NPC defenses** -- Drop high-quality items and rare crafting components.
* **All defenses** -- Drop hundreds or thousands of resources when destroyed.
* **Non-NPC defenses** -- Players earn [**Skulls**](../Currencies/Skulls.md) for killing them.

### PvP structure Skull payout

When a non-NPC structure is destroyed in PvP, players who attacked it earn Skulls based on the structure's Gear Score:

| Structure | Skull payout |
|-----------|--------------|
| Outpost / Shipyard | 1 Skull per 500 Gear Score |
| Garrison | 1 Skull per 400 Gear Score |
| Planet | 1 Skull per 300 Gear Score |

For structure attack/defense strategy, see [Structure Busting Strategy](../../Strategy/Structure-Busting.md).

## PvE Deployment

Defenses can be deployed in **human-controlled PvE maps** but **recall automatically after 15 days**. See [Deployment](Deployment.md) for full rules.

## Structure Types

| Structure | Summary |
|-----------|---------|
| [**Planets**](Planets.md) | One per corp. Respawn option; system-wide tech; x40 Hull/Shield; Sacred Relic Shrine = alliance-wide tech. |
| [**Garrisons**](Garrisons.md) | Up to 4. Garrison Strike (area attack); x30 Hull/Shield; capture Relic Shrines. |
| [**Outposts**](Outposts.md) | Up to 8. Territory growth every 6h; x12 Hull/Shield; bonuses for corp in territory. |
| [**Shipyards**](Shipyards.md) | Up to 4. Build ships; unlock blueprints; x12 Hull/Shield. |

Corporations store [**Artifacts**](Artifacts.md) at planets and garrisons for tech bonuses; any player can study an artifact for +1 Skill Point.

For structure caps, slot tables, Equip Point formulas, and default technologies, see [Structure Reference](Structure-Reference.md).

## NPC Factions & Behavior

| Faction | Behavior | Notes |
|---------|----------|--------|
| **Human** | Non-hostile; accept deposits | Garrisons at Jump Gates can be Quick Jump targets once discovered. Human Outposts/Shipyards roam. |
| **Wyrd, Hets, Precursors, Pirates** | Hostile | Structures spawn in **PvP maps** and **Security 9 PvE** maps controlled by that faction. |
