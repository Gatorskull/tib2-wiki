# Planets

**Planets** are the economic and social hubs of *The Infinite Black 2*.

## Corporation Planets
* **Limit:** A Corporation may only own **1 Planet**.
* **Function:** Serves as the central economic hub for the Corporation.

## Unique Faction Homeworlds
Specific NPC factions have permanent Homeworlds that always respawn at the same coordinates (**7 / 7**) in their respective Security 9 maps.

| Planet Name | Faction | Map | Coordinates | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
