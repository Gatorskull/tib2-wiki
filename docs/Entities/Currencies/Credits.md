# Credits

**Credits** are the primary standard currency in *The Infinite Black 2*. They are the lifeblood of the player economy and are **fully tradable**.

## How to Obtain

Credits are primarily generated through commerce and recycling:

* **Auction House:** Selling items, resources, or equipment to other players.
* **Scrapping Junk:** Breaking down "Junk" items found during gameplay.
* **Scrapping Resources:** Scrapping piles of **Crafted Resources**.

## Uses

Credits have three main functions:

1.  **The Auction House:** Purchasing items, ships, and gear listed by other players.
2.  **Raffles:** Buying tickets for **Unique Ship** raffles.
3.  **Tech Point Conversion:** Credits can be mass-converted into Tech Points to fuel progression.

!!! example "Conversion Rate"
    **1,000 Credits** $\rightarrow$ **1 Tech Point**
