# Credits

**Credits** are the primary standard currency in *The Infinite Black 2*. They are the lifeblood of the player economy and are **fully tradable** between players.

## How to Obtain

Credits are generated through commerce and recycling:

* **Auction House** -- Sell items, resources, or equipment to other players.
* **Scrapping** -- Break down "Junk" items or scrap piles of crafted resources.
* **Harvesting and resource stacks** -- Harvest resources, create stacks from deposited resources, then sell or scrap those stacks.
* **PvE busts and farming** -- NPC structure/planet busts and open-world farming can produce items worth scrapping or selling.
* **Trading** -- Direct trades with other players (including Skulls for Credits).

## Uses

| Use | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Auction House** | Buy items, ships, and gear listed by other players. |
| **Raffles** | Buy tickets for unique ship raffles. |
| **Tech Point conversion** | Convert Credits into Tech Points to fuel progression. |

!!! example "Conversion rate"
    **1,000 Credits** -> **1 Tech Point** (at any time)

## Market caveats

TIB2 does not provide a durable market-history table in this wiki. Player prices move over time, and old Discord prices should be treated as dated observations rather than current reference values.

For live prices:

- Ask active players or your corporation.
- Check current market listings.
- Treat unusual market-chat values as context, not a rule.
