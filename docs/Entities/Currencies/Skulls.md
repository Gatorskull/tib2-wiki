# Skulls

**Skulls** are a currency and crafting resource awarded to Captains **only from Player-vs-Player (PvP)** activity. They are **tradable** and used throughout the game for high-end progression.

## How to Obtain

### The Arena

[**The Arena**](<../../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Arena.md>) is a controlled PvP environment with balanced teams and no Artifacts or Corporation Skills. Rounds run every few hours and award Skulls.

* **Minimum:** One [**ship**](../../Ships/index.md) with [**Gear Score**](../../Items/index.md) of **1,000** and no free starting gear. Some arenas require more than one ship.
* **Winning:** The team with the most kills wins. High kill counts can give bonus rewards even if your team loses.
* **No durability loss** on death in The Arena; gear loses only 1% durability, and only half the time.

Access via **Captain Panel -> ARENA** when registration is open. Arena starts every few hours at the top of the hour.

### Open-world PvP and structure warfare

* **Open-world PvP** -- Destroying enemy player ships in non-safe sectors.
* **Structure warfare** -- **Bonus Skulls** are awarded for **defending** or **attacking** non-NPC [**defense units**](../Structures/index.md). Join a [**Corporation**](../Corporations/index.md) and get involved in the [**Alliance Wars**](../Corporations/Alliances.md) to earn more.
* **Structure kills** -- Structure kill payout is based on [structure Gear Score](../Structures/index.md): Outposts/Shipyards pay 1 Skull per 500 GS, Garrisons pay 1 per 400 GS, and Planets pay 1 per 300 GS.

#### Player kill credit

Open-world player-kill Skull credit works this way:

| Condition | Skull credit |
|-----------|--------------|
| You get the killing shot | You gain 1 Skull. |
| You attacked the target while it was in combat | You also gain 1 Skull when it dies. |
| Your corporation gets the kill | Your corporation gains 1 Skull. |

Additional observed bonuses can apply:

| Bonus condition | Extra Skulls |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Weekend event | +1 |
| Daily double-Skulls PvP event | +1 |
| Defending an engaged structure | +2 |
| Attacking an engaged structure | +1 |
| Fighting at an engaged structure against a high-Gear-Score killed ship | +1 |

!!! note "Needs confirmation"
    Open-world bonus values can change. Verify in game after PvP reward changes.

## Uses

| Use | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| [**Ship crafting**](../../Ships/Ship-Crafting.md) | Required (with [Relics](Relics.md)) to build advanced ship blueprints at corp Shipyards. |
| [**Elite Ranks**](../../Ships/Elite-Ranks.md) | Spent on the Unit Inspect Panel to upgrade ships and defense structures. |
| **Ship durability repair** | Use the **REPAIR** button on the ship inspection panel to restore durability (consumes Skulls). |

### Ship Elite Rank costs (Skulls)

| Rank | Cost |
|------|------|
| 1 | 100 |
| 2 | 500 |
| 3 | 1,000 |
| 4 | 2,000 |
| 5 | 3,000 |

*Defense unit Elite Rank costs are **5x** the ship costs.*

## Market Value

Skulls are needed for crafting and Elite Ranks. Exact prices are market-dependent and should be checked in game rather than treated as fixed wiki data.
