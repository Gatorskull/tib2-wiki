# Currencies

  

## Currency List

There are four primary currencies used in *The Infinite Black 2*.

  

* [**Credits**](Credits.md) - The standard tradable currency. Used for trading, raffles, and converting to Tech Points.

* [**Skulls**](Skulls.md) - The high-value PvP currency. Tradable and used for ship crafting and elite ranks.

* [**Tech Points**](Tech%20Points.md) - Bound progression currency.

* [**Star Shards**](Star%20Shards.md) - Premium currency.

  
  

**Tradability**

Only **Credits** and **Skulls** are directly tradable between players. Tech Points and Star Shards are bound to your account.

  

!!! info "Market Watch: Current Trade Ratios"
