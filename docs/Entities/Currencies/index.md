# Currencies

*The Infinite Black 2* uses several currencies and key resources. Only **Credits** and **Skulls** are tradable between players; the rest are bound to your account.

## Primary Currencies

| Currency | Tradable? | Summary |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| [**Credits**](Credits.md) | Yes | Standard economy currency. Trading, raffles, converting to Tech Points. |
| [**Skulls**](Skulls.md) | Yes | PvP currency. Ship crafting, [Elite Ranks](../../Ships/Elite-Ranks.md), ship durability repair. |
| [**Tech Points**](Tech Points.md) | No | Progression and utility. Item upgrades, corps, bank slots, Quick Jump, store. |
| [**Star Shards**](Star Shards.md) | No | Premium currency. Cosmetics only (skins, trails, avatars). |

## Crafting & Territory Resources

| Resource | Tradable? | Summary |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| [**Relics**](Relics.md) | Yes | Rare ship-crafting component. From Alliance Wars (shrines), Arena, level rewards, loot. |

!!! info "Market note"
    The exchange rate between Credits and Skulls fluctuates with player activity (often around **700–800 Credits per Skull**). See the [Auction House](Credits.md#uses) and in-game market for current rates.
