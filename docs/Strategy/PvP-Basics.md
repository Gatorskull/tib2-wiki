# PvP Basics

PvP advice changes with player habits, fleet size, daily events, and balance patches. Treat this page as community guidance, not game rules.

For stable rules, see [Player vs Player](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/index.md>), [Arena](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Arena.md>), [Fleet PvP Rules](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Fleet-PvP.md>), and [Structure Warfare Rules](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Structure Busts.md>).

## Before Entering PvP Space

Check:

- Whether open-world PvP is active today.
- Whether your route passes through enemy-controlled maps.
- Whether your ship durability and item durability are acceptable.
- Whether you are following a fleet driver or traveling alone.
- Whether you understand the local sector ship counts.

## Common PvP Habits

- Join an active corporation and follow alliance calls when possible.
- Turn on sector ship counts before large fights.
- Learn assist leader and follow workflows before the fight starts.
- Keep structure warfare, Arena, and open-world hunting separated in your head; they have different risks and reward rules.
