# Fleet PvP Strategy

Fleet PvP strategy changes with players, builds, and balance patches. Treat this page as community guidance, not game rules.

For stable mechanics, see [Fleet PvP Rules](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Fleet-PvP.md>), [Sectors](../World/Maps/Sectors.md), [Skulls](../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md), and [Interdictor Carrier](../Ships/Interdictor-Carrier.md).

## Stack habits

Common fleet expectations from training data and gootchat:

- Do not join a full stack if it would push the sector over the alliance limit.
- If collision starts, move out or swap stacks when called.
- Drivers and copilots may split ships across multiple stacks to keep the fight active without wiping the fleet to collision damage.
- Keep **Display local sector ship counts** enabled before joining large fleet activity.

## Sort calls

Commanders call enemy sort orders so allies see targets in the same order and focus fire more reliably.

Common examples from training data and gootchat:

| Sort call | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Smallest hull first** | Focus low-hull ships first. |
| **Largest hull first** | Focus high-hull ships first. |
| **Smallest size first** | Focus smaller ships first. |
| **Largest size first** | Focus larger ships first. |
| **Most in danger** | Focus ships already close to death. |
| **Worst gear** | Focus weaker gear-score targets. |
| **First to arrive** | Focus ships by arrival/order list. |

Sort calls are operational instructions, not universal strategy. Follow the live fleet call unless your commander tells you otherwise.

## Driver survival

Gootchat repeatedly emphasizes that driver ships should avoid being at the top of common enemy sort lists. Practical driver-building ideas from those messages include:

- Blend into common enemy sorts instead of standing out as the easiest target.
- Avoid being the lowest hull, worst gear, or most obvious high-value target.
- Consider strong grapple resist, critical resist, stealth tools, and enough survivability to survive focused pressure.
- Use copilots when possible: one player can drive while another watches collision, sort calls, refill links, and questions.

## Interdictors and movement pressure

[Interdictors](../Ships/Interdictor-Carrier.md) can slow enemy movement and targeting in affected sectors. Move-speed overstacking can help counter enemy Interdictor penalties, while [Gravity Drives](../Items/Gravity-Drives.md) negate the movement penalty for ships that equip them.
