## Notes From Current Sources

- Wyrd shield-heavy farming fleets are repeatedly described as strong, but exact shield and capacitor formulas still need confirmation.
- Drone Master and Prospector both appear in farming notes, but their exact interaction with [Harvester drones](../Items/Drones.md#harvester-drones) and Freighter needs data.
- Advanced Propulsion appears often in carrier movement discussions. Use [movement caps](../Ships/Size-And-Targeting.md) and Interdictor pressure rules as the reference baseline.
- Meta build labels should be dated or source-labeled when added.
