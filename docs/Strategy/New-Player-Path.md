# New Player Path

This is a practical route for early progression. Treat it as guidance, not a required path.

For stable mechanics and click paths, see [How To Play](../Getting-Started/How-To-Play.md), [Common Workflows](../Getting-Started/Common-Workflows.md), [FAQ](../Getting-Started/FAQ.md), and [Recommended Settings](../Getting-Started/Settings.md).

## Early Priorities

1. Follow the first mission and explore Sol.
2. Reach 100% Sol exploration to earn another ship.
3. Deploy the second ship.
4. Join an active corporation.
5. Turn on useful settings like sector ship counts and sector names.
6. Learn daily gifts and basic repair workflows.

## Why Corporation Comes Early

Corporations provide access to:

- Shared technology and skills.
- Shipyards and ship construction.
- Group PvE and PvP calls.
- Structure defense and alliance activity.
- Experienced players who can sanity-check expensive choices.
