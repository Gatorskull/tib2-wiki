# Structure Busting Strategy

Structure busting strategy changes with fleet composition, defender behavior, map layout, and balance patches. Treat this page as community guidance, not game rules.

For stable mechanics, see [Structure Warfare Rules](<../Gameplay/Activities/Player vs Player (PVP)/Structure Busts.md>), [Structures](../Entities/Structures/index.md), [Deployment](../Entities/Structures/Deployment.md), and [Skulls](../Entities/Currencies/Skulls.md).

## Practical notes

Community guidance from gootchat repeatedly treats structure busts as fleet operations:

- Expect drivers, assist leaders, sort calls, and collision management.
- Watch shield timers and Love Day / War Day timing.
- Keep enough ships spread or rotating to avoid collision damage.
- Defenders often use Garrisons and structure positioning to control lanes and force attackers into awkward sectors.

These are strategic patterns, not hard rules. See [Fleet PvP Strategy](Fleet-PvP.md) for driver and sort-order basics.

## Strategy caveats

Gootchat includes several tactical ideas such as low-Gear-Score-but-effective structures, lane setups, rotating smaller stacks, and using garrisons for Skull generation. Keep those labeled as community strategy unless verified as stable mechanics.
