## Wyrd Shield Notes

Gootchat has several useful Wyrd shield observations, but they should remain strategy/source notes until verified:

- Shield-heavy Wyrd ships are repeatedly recommended for farming durability.
- One source note says Wyrd shield capacitors are "at and above 10% of max."
- Another note describes an E3 Wyrd behavior where, while the ship has shields, a shot has a 10% chance to be completely soaked/negated.
- Large shield pools appear to increase periodic shield restoration in observed examples.

These are strong leads for testing, not final formulas.
